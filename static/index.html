📊 Season Structure

Two Halves: The season is split into two halves at Round 12 (British GP).
Mid-Season Draft: After Round 12, teams can re-draft their 5 drivers for the second half.
Total Score: Your final score is the sum of H1 + H2 points.

DNS (Did Not Start): 0 points
